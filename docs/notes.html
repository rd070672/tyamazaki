Elastocaloric cooling

Elastocaloric cooling is a promising technology that utilizes the temperature change of a material caused by an applied mechanical stress to produce cooling. The technology has the potential to provide a more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly alternative to traditional vapor compression refrigeration systems.
